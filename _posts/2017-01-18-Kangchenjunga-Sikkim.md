title: Kangchenjunga, Sikkim
---

This massive mountain is Kangchenjunga. It is the third tallest
mountain on Earth, and is part of the Sikkim Himalayas in North-East India.
We encountered cloudy weather throughout the week, but were gifted 
with this majestic view from Pelling, which is one of the nearest villages from the
mountain.
